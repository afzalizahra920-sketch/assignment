what is a color wheel?

the color wheel    is a visual representationof colors arranged in a circle.
it shows how color relate to each other,how they mix ,blend and contrat.

primary colors

primary color are the base color can not be created by mixing other colors.

  • red
  • blue
  • yellow
all other colors on the wheel are made from these three.

red

red is a warm and strong color .
it is often associated with:

  1. energy
  2. passion
  3. love
  4. strength

blue

blue is a cool and calming color.
it is often associated with:

  1. trust
  2. peace
  3. loyalty
  4. stability

yellow

yellow is a bright and cheerful color.
it is often associated with:

  1. happiness
  2. hope
  3. sunlight
  4. energy

secondary colors

secondary colors are created by mixing two primary colos:

mix

             red+blue              blue+yellow              yellow+red

result

              purple/violet              green              orange
